Pizza

Description

A pizza is a round, oven-baked dish featuring a chewy or crisp crust topped with a layer of tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, or herbs; when baked, the crust becomes golden, the cheese turns gooey and slightly browned, and the combined aromas create a savory, satisfying meal enjoyed in countless styles around the world.

Ingredients

  • dough
  • tomato sauce
